GLITCH ART


The "Fashion" Series



The series of images is about fashion and the glitch kind of portrays how we as a society are affected by fashion. It's a message of how people see you differently based on what you wear and what clothes you have.



For this project there were utilization of two different glitching methods. One using audio while the other was adjusting the image code.

This image is a glitch using the data bending technique with a text editor.




The next two images are glitches using the data moshing technique with audacity.

I personally liked data moshing more than data bending because of how easy it is to execute and how fluid the glitches come out with that technique.
